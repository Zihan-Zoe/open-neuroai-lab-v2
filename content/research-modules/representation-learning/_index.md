# Representation Learning

This module studies how biological and artificial systems learn internal representations of the world.

## Core questions

- What is a representation?
- How are latent spaces formed and updated?
- Can neural population activity be modeled as trajectories on manifolds?
- How do AI representation learning methods relate to neural coding?
- Can representations transfer across models, tasks, animals, or brain regions?
- How can replay and prediction support stable learning?
