Research modules are the main knowledge structure of **Open NeuroAI Lab**.

Each module integrates concepts, related courses, curated paper notes, mini projects, datasets, code templates, open questions, and links to publishable projects.

| Module | Focus |
| --- | --- |
| [Brain-Computer Interfaces](brain-computer-interfaces/) | Neural signals, decoding systems, BCI modalities, neural engineering, and interface design. |
| [Representation Learning](representation-learning/) | Latent spaces, manifolds, transfer, alignment, predictive learning, JEPA, replay, and neural dynamics. |
