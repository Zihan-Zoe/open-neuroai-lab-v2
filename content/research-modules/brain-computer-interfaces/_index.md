# Brain-Computer Interfaces

This module studies how neural signals can be recorded, decoded, interpreted, and used for interaction with external systems.

## Core questions

- What types of neural signals are suitable for decoding?
- How do EEG, ECoG, spikes, LFP, fMRI, and functional ultrasound differ?
- What makes a decoding system safe, stable, and generalizable?
- How can AI models improve neural decoding?
- What are the limits of non-invasive and invasive BCI?
